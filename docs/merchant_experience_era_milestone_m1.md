### What M1 delivered

1. **Governed knowledge pipeline** — Truth → Evidence → Proof → Decision → Explanation → Producer → Routing → Projection → Surface.
2. **Four certified routing consumers** — no merchant-facing surface re-decides platform knowledge on daily paths.
3. **Merchant Home operating center** — five-section morning story (achievements → attention → understanding → nav).
4. **Product review baseline** — first executive merchant experience verdict (Review Board V1).
5. **Cohesion standard** — MEC-1…MEC-7 + Whisper Principle for Product Excellence work.

### Transition

**CartFlow now transitions from platform construction to Product Excellence.**

Next work optimizes **merchant feeling and habit** (Cohesion Program phases), not knowledge ownership. Architecture extends infrastructure as consumers only — per Permanent Architectural Rule.
