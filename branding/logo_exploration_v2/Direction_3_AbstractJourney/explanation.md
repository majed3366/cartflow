# Direction 3 — Abstract Journey

**Phase:** Logo exploration v2 (concept)  
**Metaphor:** Presence deepens into resolution — layered progression.

---

## What you are looking at

Three **overlapping rounded squares**, back to front:

1. Light gray — prior presence on the path  
2. Indigo — transition / decision weight  
3. Dark solid — outcome  

No connecting lines. No dots-and-lines timeline. Depth and overlap suggest **journey through time**, not a network diagram.

---

## Brand meaning alignment

| Stage | Expression in mark |
|-------|-------------------|
| Customer on path | Rear, lightest layer |
| Hesitation / understanding | Middle layer gains color and weight |
| Act / recover | Front layer — full solidity |

Maps to **Understand → Decide → Act → Recover** as **material depth**, not a flowchart.

---

## Design reference (not copy)

Studied **Notion** for soft geometric stacking and **Linear** for flat precision. CartFlow uses **three layers only** — enough for story, not enough for architecture.

---

## Strengths

- Richer **narrative** without literal icons  
- Diagonal overlap feels **dynamic** in marketing layouts  
- Color progression uses brand tokens (`#E2E8F0` → `#6366F1` → `#1E293B`)  
- No ecommerce or chat association  

---

## Weaknesses

- Three layers risk reading as “loading steps” or “progress indicator”  
- Busier than Directions 1 and 2 at 16px  
- Closer to diagram language than intended — overlap must stay subtle  
- Harder to animate distinctly without looking like a skeleton loader
